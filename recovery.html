Program Cleanup & Redevelopment

Recover control of an underperforming program.

We help organizations identify root causes, stabilize operations, correct compliance risks, and rebuild a sustainable operating model.

Common warning signs

  • Claims are denied or not submitted consistently
  • Staff use spreadsheets because the EHR does not support the workflow
  • Documentation is incomplete, inconsistent, or difficult to audit
  • Roles overlap and tasks fall between departments
  • Authorizations, referrals, or service starts are delayed
  • Leadership cannot easily see capacity, status, or financial performance

Program Recovery Assessment

A structured review of contracts, workflows, staffing, documentation, systems, claims, controls, and reporting. Deliverables include a risk-ranked gap analysis and phased corrective action plan.

Recovery work can be scoped to one program, one workflow, or an enterprise-wide CalAIM operating model.
