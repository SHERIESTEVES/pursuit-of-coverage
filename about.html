Getting to know Pursuit of Coverage

Pursuit of Coverage is a California-focused consulting and training company supporting community-based organizations, health care providers, and emerging CalAIM programs.

Our work focuses on the space where policy, operations, documentation, billing, technology, and member experience meet. We identify gaps, rebuild processes, and equip teams to sustain improvements.
