Practical Implementation Support

From policy requirements to everyday operations

CalAIM programs often struggle because policies, contracts, EHR workflows, billing rules, and staff responsibilities are not aligned. We connect those pieces into one usable operating model.

  • Operational gap assessments and corrective action plans
  • Policies, procedures, templates, and decision trees
  • Case management and EHR workflow redesign
  • Authorization, claims, denial, and audit readiness support
  • Dashboards, quality controls, and leadership reporting
